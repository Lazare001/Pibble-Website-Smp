Server Rules

These rules ensure everyone has a fun and fair experience on Pibble SMP. Please read them carefully and follow them at all times.

✓

Be Respectful

Treat all players with respect. No harassment, bullying, or discriminatory behavior.

✓

No Griefing

Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without permission.

✓

Fair Play

No cheating, hacking, or using unfair advantages. Play fair and have fun.

✓

Keep it Appropriate

Use appropriate language and avoid controversial topics that may offend others.

✓

Report Issues

If you see someone breaking rules, report it to staff through Discord.

✓

Have Fun

Most importantly, enjoy your time on the server and help make it a great community!
